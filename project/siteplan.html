InspireHub Site Plan

Site Name

InspireHub - A place where users receive daily motivation, practical tools for personal growth, and encouragement to lead their best lives. The name reflects a hub or center for daily inspiration and transformation.

Optional domain availability: inspirehubdaily.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of InspireHub is to provide motivation and support for individuals interested in self-development. The site will include daily quotes, curated articles, personal challenges, a goal tracker, and more to assist users in their growth journey.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find motivational quotes and personal development content daily?
  • How do I track my progress on a 30-day challenge or personal goal?
  • Which book should I read next to boost my productivity and mindset?

Color Schema

  • #E0F7FA (Light Blue) – background color for main sections
  • #004D40 (Teal Green) – used for headers and buttons
  • #FFFFFF (White) – background for cards and readable content
  • #FF9800 (Orange) – used for accent highlights (like quote borders or CTAs)

Typography

  • Roboto – used for body text, clean and readable
  • Playfair Display – used for headings to create a sense of elegance and focus
